Chicken Alfredo Recipe

This one goes out to all the "alfredo warriors".

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low. Add butter to the pan and let it melt. Stir in heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper. Cook until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.
  5. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and stir to coat with the Alfredo sauce.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the pan, tossing to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
