Pizza Recipe

"Pizza time."

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Black olives
  • Italian seasoning
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature for pizza.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
  4. Evenly distribute mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and black olives on top of the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle Italian seasoning over the toppings for extra flavor.
  6. Drizzle a bit of olive oil over the pizza for a golden crust.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a moment, slice, and enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
