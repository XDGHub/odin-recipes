Ramen Bowl

"Look at all that low-res, savory goodness!"

Ingredients:

  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of shredded carrots
  • 1 cup of baby spinach
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • Green onions for garnish
  • Sriracha or chili oil (optional, for extra spice)

Steps:

  1. Boil the ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. In a separate pot, heat the chicken or vegetable broth.
  3. Add mushrooms, shredded carrots, minced garlic, and grated ginger to the broth. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil for flavor.
  5. Add the cooked ramen noodles to the broth and vegetables.
  6. Place baby spinach in the serving bowls and ladle the hot broth over the noodles and vegetables.
  7. Garnish with halved boiled eggs and chopped green onions.
  8. Optional: Add Sriracha or chili oil for extra spice.
